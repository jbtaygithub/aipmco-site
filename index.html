Why AI efforts stall in delivery

Most teams add AI on top of broken delivery systems. The result is faster documentation of the same problems.

01

AI layered on top of broken workflows

Without workflow clarity, AI amplifies inconsistency and rework.

02

Decision latency + unclear ownership

If inputs and decision rights aren’t explicit, delivery slows—AI can’t fix that.

03

PMs drowning in status while delivery slips anyway

Tooling helps document the drift. Systems thinking prevents it.
